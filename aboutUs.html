NIGANTU is a Fashion Tech Company that aims to provide world's best artificial intelligence learning platform for fashion aspirants across the globe to improve their skills through our well designed sustainable fashion curriculum, we help designers solve their most challenging fashion innovations by connecting them with industry expertise and thereby transform business ideas, personalities and skills of many. It has a scope of substance stages that inspire, illuminate, instruct and engage individuals across dialects, societies and geologies.​

Fashion is more than just tackling textiles or items of apparel. It involves addressing the entire fashion system. This includes tackling interdependent socioeconomic, economic and environmental processes. Sustainable fashion is a necessary trend and mechanism to promote a changeover of fashion goods . This needs collaboration among various disciplines and sound decision making during product design. NIGANTU tries to infuse sustainable fashion into fashion aspirants through its courses which in turn leads future retail industry towards greater environmental sustainability and economic justice.​

The most effective and successful people in the industry are the ones who aim to be creative. NIGANTU Initiates to unleash creativity from young minds, instill courage and provide a connected platform where they can create and present ideas directly from mobiles/Personal computers​

We aim to provide products and services inline with the industry standards while also paying undivided attention to our users by bridging their gaps and providing potential opportunities to bring innovative ideas into existence and thereby increase customer loyalty and market believability.​
